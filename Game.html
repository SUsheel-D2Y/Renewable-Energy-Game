Renewable Energy Sudoku

B V Raju Institute of Technology

Department of Chemical Engineering

Game Instructions:

Green Technology Sudoku is a fun and educational game that combines the logic of Sudoku with symbols representing renewable energy sources such as the sun, wind, and water.

Note: The Easy level uses 5 symbols, and the Hard level uses 7 symbols. Choose carefully, as the complexity increases with more symbols. Strategize wisely to win!

The objective is to fill the grid with symbols such that each row, column, and grid contains all the symbols without repetition.

Good luck and enjoy the challenge!
